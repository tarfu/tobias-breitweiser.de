25.07.2017 Ötztal Wildspitze (try)

 Posted on July 25, 2017  |  Tobias Breitwieser

On the 25.07.2017 my Partner (Daniel) went out to summit the Wildspitze in Vent (Ötztal, Austria) over the normal route through Mittelkarjoch. Our base was the Breslauer Hut. Weather conditions where far from good with -2C to -5C, a chill temperature of -15C, snow, clouds and heavy winds. On the Taschachferner the visibility was partly down to a few meters and no tracks were visible. In this conditions I broke into a not visible crevasse and hit my knee hard a few meters before the end of the glacier. [Read More]
